Carolyn Ellingson is a San Francisco artist who is known for her strong abstract images and clean, decisive and exciting color. She has her origins in Chicago and Minneapolis where she earned advanced degrees from The University of Minnesota. She moved to California in 1983 and maintains a studio at Hunter's Point Shipyard. Carolyn creates non-representational oil and acrylic paintings, monotypes, pastels and watercolors.

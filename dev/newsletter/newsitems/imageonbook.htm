Good news! One of my images, Mastermind, is going to be reproduced on the cover of a book. A professor of literature in Cordoba, Spain, Antonio Ruiz, contacted me to ask permission to use this image on his new book about the 19th century Canadian novel which is to be published this October. Of course I was honored to have been asked and did give my permission.

Professor Ruiz found me by searching in Yahoo and typing in the "art section": abstract paintings, getting Arts > Visual Arts > Painting > Artists > Personal Exhibits (and there was my website with the image he liked so much).

"Two Reds, #12" - by Carolyn Ellingson

Beautiful cadmium red monotype 8" square image on Arches Cover paper, 20" high x 15" wide--lots of white space. Can also be cropped to 15" square for framing in a square format, signed on border by me, the artist. Unframed so easy for me to ship in a tube. Or if you live in the San Francisco Bay Area you have the option to pick it up in my studio.

Shipping
Buyer pays actual packing, shipping, insurance costs from San Francisco, CA. Contact me for an estimate. Option to pick up piece at artist's Hunters Point studio for buyers residing in San Francisco Bay Area. Larger pieces must be packed and shipped professionally. The Packaging Store in San Francisco has packed and shipped my artwork for many years and I recommend their services for this purpose. I'll make every effort to pack and ship smaller pieces and unframed prints myself to keep buyer's packing and shipping costs down.

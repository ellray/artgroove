"In Full Bloom" - by Carolyn Ellingson

What a bright and beautiful painting. It is typical of my style when the paint just rolls off my brush easily. If you want to feel good every day, hang this painting where you can see it! Painting is 24" high x 18" wide and is signed on the back by the artist. It would be best to have this painting professionally packed and shipped. Or if you live in the San Francisco Bay Area, you can pick it up at my San Francisco studio.

"Opalescent Garden, II" - by Carolyn Ellingson

This very unusual NEW large acrylic iridescent painting on canvas has layers and layers of iridescent and some other types of acrylic paint. These layers catch the light in all sorts of ways. Golden's iridescent paints contain titanium dioxide coated mica particles. From different perspectives the colors flip between a bright opalescent color and its alternative. The position of the color to light determines what you will see--direct light gives a different effect than indirect light. Painting is 36" high x 48" wide and is signed on the back by the artist. This is a large painting and must be professionally packed and shipped. Or if you live in the San Francisco Bay Area, you can pick it up at my San Francisco studio.
